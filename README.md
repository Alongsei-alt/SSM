# SSM# SSM Strategy Team Project

## Project Overview

This repository contains our completed team project for the **Strategy / SSM course**.

The project focuses on analyzing the strategy of a selected company using strategic management frameworks discussed in class. The goal is to understand the company’s external environment, internal resources, competitive advantage, business strategy, corporate strategy, and future strategic options.

## Project Objective

The objective of this project is to apply strategic analysis tools to a real company and develop evidence-based managerial recommendations.

The project addresses questions such as:

- How attractive is the industry?
- What external trends affect the company?
- What does the competitive environment look like?
- What are the company’s key resources and capabilities?
- How does the company create competitive advantage?
- What are its business and corporate strategic choices?
- What recommendations can be made for future strategy?

## Analysis Structure

The project is organized into three main parts.

### 1. External Analysis

This part analyzes the company’s business environment and industry conditions.

It may include:

- Industry attractiveness
- Broad environmental trends
- Five Forces analysis
- Strategic groups
- Industry comparison with the economic average
- Opportunities and threats

### 2. Internal Analysis

This part analyzes the company’s internal strengths, weaknesses, resources, and capabilities.

It may include:

- Competitive advantage
- Cost position
- Sources of differentiation
- Business strategy
- Corporate strategy
- Diversification
- Vertical integration
- Sustainability approach

### 3. Conclusions and Recommendations

This final part summarizes the main findings and provides strategic recommendations.

The recommendations are based on the external and internal analysis and are intended to support the company’s future strategic development.

## Deliverables

The completed project includes:

- Final PowerPoint presentation
- Properly cited sources
- Strategic analysis frameworks
- Managerial conclusions and recommendations
